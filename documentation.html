This section provides details on the datasets used in our visualisations and their sources.

Datasets

Dataset Description Source
World's Pollution Index Data on live rankings of the most polluted countries and their major cities (in City-Country pairs) sorted from the worst to the cleanest. IQAir - World Air Quality Ranking
Exposure to PM2.5 Fine Particles (OECD) Data on the exposure levels of different countries to PM2.5 Fine Particles. Exposure to Air Pollution
Mortality, Morbidity and Welfare Cost from Exposure to Air Pollution (OECD) Data on the health impacts of exposure to air pollution. DALYs lost per 1,000 inhabitants due to exposure to air pollution
Disease Burden by Risk Factor in 2021 (Southeast Asia) Data on risk factors contributing to disease burden. Underlying Risk Factors to Chronic Diseases
Attribution of Air Pollution to Chronic Disease Deaths in 2021 (Southeast Asia) Data on risk factors contributing to disease burden. Cause-specific Disease Burden
Death by Risk Factors in 2021 (World) Data on the number of deaths globally by diferent risk factors. Main Contributors to Death in 2021 (World)
Number of Deaths Attributable to PM2.5 in 2021 (Southeast Asia) Data on number of deaths caused by air pollution. Death Counts from Air Pollution (World)
Source of Air Pollution in 2021 (Global) Data on the sources of air pollution on global scale. Source Contributions to Air Pollution in 2021 (Global)
Source of Air Pollution in 2021 (Southeast Asia) Data on the sources of air pollution in Southeast Asia region. Source Contributions to Air Pollution in 2021 (Southeast Asia)
