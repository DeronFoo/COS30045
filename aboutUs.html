About Us

We are a dedicated team of researchers and developers working towards visualizing data for better understanding and actionable insights. Our goal is to bridge the gap between raw data and informed decision-making by creating interactive, user-friendly visualizations.

This project focuses on showcasing the burden of diseases caused by air pollution and other risk factors in Southeast Asia. We aim to provide clarity and transparency in data interpretation for researchers, policymakers, and the general public.
